Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

1. Overview

Pulzivo Analytics ("we", "us", or "our") is a privacy-first analytics platform. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws.

2. Data We Collect

When you use Pulzivo (as a customer)

  • Account data: Email address, name, and password (hashed) when you sign up.
  • Billing data: Processed securely by our payment provider. We do not store card numbers.
  • Usage data: Feature usage within the dashboard to improve our product.

When your visitors use a site running the Pulzivo SDK

  • Page views & events: URLs visited, clicks, scroll depth, and custom events you configure.
  • Technical data: Browser type, device type, screen resolution, country (from IP — IP is never stored).
  • Session data: An anonymous session ID generated per visit. No persistent cross-site tracking.

We do not use cookies. We do not fingerprint users. We do not sell data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Pulzivo Analytics service.
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset).
  • To detect abuse and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To generate aggregated, anonymised analytics reports for our customers.

4. Data Retention

Analytics event data is retained for the duration of your active subscription. On account deletion, all associated event data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Account data (email, etc.) is deleted immediately on request.

5. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate Pulzivo:

  • Google Cloud / App Engine — backend hosting
  • MongoDB Atlas — database hosting
  • Payment processor — billing (card data never touches our servers)

These providers are contractually required to handle data securely and in accordance with applicable law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@pulzivo.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. GDPR & Cookieless Compliance

The Pulzivo SDK is designed to be used without a cookie consent banner. It does not set cookies, does not store IP addresses, and does not create persistent cross-site user profiles. You are responsible for ensuring your own use of the SDK complies with local laws.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice in the dashboard. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at privacy@pulzivo.com
